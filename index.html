About PumpCat

PumpCat is a community-driven meme token built to unite fun, utility, and hype within the Solana ecosystem. Our mission is simple: bring the pump back to Solana memes through transparency, strong community engagement, and consistent marketing pushes. The community is the project — and every member has a paw in shaping the future of PumpCat.
